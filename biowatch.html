About

BioWatch is an AI-driven solution for tracking invasive insect species in real time, using computer vision to enable rapid identification and mapping of outbreaks.

Problem Statement

Traditional monitoring of invasive species is slow and labor-intensive. BioWatch addresses the need for a real-time, accessible tool that empowers researchers and citizen scientists to report sightings quickly.

Process

We began with user research and requirement gathering, moved to wireframing and prototyping in Figma, then built the mobile interface and integrated an AI model for image recognition.

Pain Points

  • Collecting a large, labeled dataset of insect images.
  • Optimizing the AI model for speed on mobile devices.
  • Simplifying complex AI results into user-friendly feedback.

Final Product

The delivered iOS app lets users snap a photo of an insect, receive an instant species ID, and plot the sighting on a shared map dashboard for researchers.
